Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the initial consultation cost?

Our initial 30-minute consultation is completely free. This allows you to discuss your case, understand your legal options, and determine if we're the right fit for your needs without any financial commitment.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

We typically can schedule consultations within 2-3 business days. For urgent matters, we offer same-day consultations when possible. Emergency situations are prioritized immediately.

Do you handle cases outside of Delhi?

Yes, we represent clients across India. While our main office is in New Delhi, we can handle cases in various High Courts and the Supreme Court. Video consultations are available for clients who cannot visit our office.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring any relevant documents such as marriage certificates, court papers, financial records, and correspondence with the other party. A detailed timeline of events is also helpful. We'll provide a comprehensive checklist when you schedule your appointment.

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. All communications with our firm are protected by attorney-client privilege. We maintain strict confidentiality protocols and never share your information without your explicit consent, except as required by law.
